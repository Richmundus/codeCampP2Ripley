Elen Ripply

Ellen Ripley, portrayed by Sigourney Weaver, is a resilient and courageous character in the "Alien" franchise. She defies expectations, becoming a symbol of female empowerment as she battles the relentless Xenomorphs with determination and intellect.

Events

  • 2122: Survived the Nostromo incident: Ripley encountered and successfully evaded the Xenomorph creature aboard the commercial towing vessel Nostromo in the first film, "Alien" (1979).
  • 2179: Survived LV-426 mission: Ripley participated in the mission to LV-426 as an advisor in the film "Aliens" (1986). She confronted multiple Xenomorphs, rescued Newt, and survived the intense battles against the Xenomorph Queen and her offspring.
  • 2179: Defeated the Xenomorph Queen: Ripley fought a climactic battle with the Xenomorph Queen on LV-426, utilizing a Power Loader to disable and expel the Queen into space.
  • 2379: Cloned and Resurrection: In "Alien: Resurrection" (1997), it is revealed that Ripley was cloned and brought back to life approximately 200 years after her death. She displayed enhanced strength, reflexes, and acidic blood due to genetic modifications.
  • 2379: Escaped the USM Auriga: Ripley, along with a group of mercenaries, faced a new breed of Xenomorphs aboard the USM Auriga and managed to escape the doomed spaceship.
  • 2379: Sacrifice and Destruction: In a final act of bravery, Ripley sacrificed herself by falling into a furnace, causing the destruction of the remaining Xenomorphs and preventing their further exploitation.
